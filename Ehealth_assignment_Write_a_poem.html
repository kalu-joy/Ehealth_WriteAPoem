A SONNET LETTER

Dear sunshine
You once walked these paths
You know the distance, if you do the maths
Emerging with marks and bruises
maybe taken for a ride, not cruises
This I tell you
there's always something new
These paths are still not without holes
But the good thing! There are lights shinning from poles
revealing a true heart
not punching pins at you like a dart.
Oh! This twelveth line of my letter
I skip the thirteeneth to say now - not later,
I LOVE YOU SUNSHINE.

By Israel Akpan.
